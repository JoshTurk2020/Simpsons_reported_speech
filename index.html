Reported Speech Game - The Simpsons

One morning, Homer woke up late for work. Marge said, "Homer, you're going to be late for work again!"

Marge told Homer that he was going to be late for work again.

Homer replied, "Don't worry, Marge. I have a plan."

Homer told Marge not to worry and that he had a plan.

At the power plant, Homer's boss, Mr. Burns, was angry. "Simpson, you're late! Get to work now!" Mr. Burns shouted.

Mr. Burns shouted at Homer, telling him he was late and ordering him to get to work immediately.

Homer said, "Yes, sir! I'll get right to it."

Homer said "Yes, sir!" and that he would get right to it.

Later, Homer snuck out of work to go to Moe's Tavern. Moe greeted him, "Hey, Homer! The usual?"

Moe greeted Homer and asked if he wanted the usual.

Homer responded, "You know it, Moe. I need a break from work."

Homer responded affirmatively to Moe and said he needed a break from work.

Back at home, Lisa was doing her homework. She asked Homer, "Dad, can you help me with my math homework?"

Lisa asked Homer if he could help her with her math homework.

Homer said, "Sure, sweetie. But first, let's watch some TV."

Homer agreed to help Lisa but suggested they watch some TV first.

Bart was skateboarding in the house. Marge yelled, "Bart, stop skateboarding inside the house!"

Marge yelled at Bart, telling him to stop skateboarding inside the house.

Bart said, "Aw, mom. I'm just practicing my moves."

Bart said "Aw, mom" and explained that he was just practicing his moves.

At the end of the day, the family gathered for dinner. Homer said, "What a day! I love you all."

Homer remarked on what a day it had been and expressed his love for his family.
